Affiliate & Partner Information

What's the difference?

Affiliates

This is for ER:LC private servers that use Ducky as their main bot. You can apply by creating a management ticket in our support server.

Partners

This is for other types of servers, such as Discord bots/services. You can apply by creating a management ticket in our support server.

Affiliate Requirements

Active Usage of Ducky

You must use Ducky as your main bot to manage your ER:LC server. This means you must use most of our features and not use different bots for features we offer.

Exceptions will be made for some features.

200+ Members & Activity

You must have an active community with at least 200 members. Your server will go through an inspection to make sure it is treated professionally.

Placing Our Advertisement

As we will place your advertisement in our server, you must also do so in your server. The advertisement can be found in the attached file.

You don't have to post it before applying.

Affiliate Benefits

Early Access

We may sometimes release features to affiliates first for bigger scale testing and feedback.

Direct Support

You can get support from our support team and developers in your server.

Partner Requirements

This will be discussed in the ticket as it can vary depending on which services you provide.
