"It doesn't work" is not a bug report

Imagine calling a mechanic, saying "my car doesn't work", and hanging up. That's what "it doesn't work" sounds like to us.

To help us help you, please provide as much detail as possible.

Provide exact steps to reproduce

List the exact steps you took that led to the issue. This helps us follow the same path to see what's going wrong.

Show, don't just tell

A picture is worth a thousand words. A video is worth a million. Show us what you're seeing.

Include relevant context

Provide details like your Server ID, or any other information that might be relevant to the issue.
